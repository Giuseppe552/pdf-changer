# How to safely share PDFs

## Know what “scrubbing” means

Scrubbing removes common metadata and interactive elements. It does **not** remove identifiers that are visible in the content.

## Prefer least exposure

- Share only the pages that are necessary.
- Remove hyperlinks and annotations if they are not needed.
- Avoid including “extra” files or attachments.

## Validate before sending

Open the scrubbed PDF and confirm:

- Content looks correct
- Links are removed (v1 removes all annotations)
- Dates are normalized

If your risk is high, consider an air‑gapped or offline workflow.
