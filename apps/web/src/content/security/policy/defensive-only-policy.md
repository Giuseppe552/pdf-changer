# Defensive-Only Security Policy

PDF Changer security content is for **privacy protection, risk reduction, and safer document handling**.

## What we publish

- Defensive guidance for reducing accidental exposure.
- Practical security workflows for individuals and teams.
- Threat-model framing and limitations, including what controls cannot do.

## What we do not publish

- Instructions intended to enable fraud, deception, or abuse.
- Evasion playbooks designed to avoid accountability for harm.
- Coercive, malicious, or non-consensual operational guidance.

## What this does not protect

- Misuse of tools or information outside defensive intent.
- Legal outcomes in specific jurisdictions.
- Risks outside document and workflow security.
