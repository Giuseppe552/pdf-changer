# Office Worker Secure Sharing

Most office incidents are preventable. Use structured steps instead of ad-hoc sharing.

## Before sending

- Confirm recipient list and remove unnecessary CCs.
- Confirm document purpose and remove extra pages.
- Scrub with `/scrub`, then open the output and verify content.

## During sending

- Prefer direct secure channels over public links.
- Add expiry and access limits when using portals.
- Keep subject lines neutral and non-identifying.

## What this does not protect

- Data copied into email body text.
- Misconfigured permissions in external sharing tools.
- Existing logs in enterprise tools you do not control.
