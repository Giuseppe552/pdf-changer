# Whistleblower Quickstart

If risk is real, keep your process simple. Complexity increases mistakes. This guide is defensive only and focused on risk reduction.

## High-level sequence

1. Define who might retaliate and what they can realistically observe.
2. Prepare files offline where possible.
3. Scrub documents locally with `/scrub`.
4. Share only with trusted recipients and minimal context.

## Safe communication habits

- Never reuse personal accounts for sensitive submissions.
- Separate daily life devices from sensitive workflows when possible.
- Prefer short factual messages. Extra detail can create unique fingerprints.

## What this does not protect

- Legal exposure from disclosure in your jurisdiction.
- Physical surveillance or pressure from known adversaries.
- Mistakes made before you started this workflow.
