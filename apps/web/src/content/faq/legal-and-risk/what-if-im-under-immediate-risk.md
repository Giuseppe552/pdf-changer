# What if I am under immediate risk?

Prioritize personal safety first, pause non-essential actions, and seek qualified legal or emergency support before continuing.

## Why this matters

When stress is high, mistakes increase. Safety and legal support come before perfect technical workflows.

## Safe default steps

1. Move to personal safety first.
2. Stop ad-hoc digital activity until you have a clear plan.
3. Contact qualified legal counsel or trusted support organizations.
4. Resume document handling only when your risk plan is clear.

## Common mistakes

- Continuing technical steps while in panic.
- Contacting random channels without checking trust.
- Leaving traces across many platforms in a short period.

## Limits

Technical tools can reduce document risk but cannot substitute for emergency planning or legal strategy.
