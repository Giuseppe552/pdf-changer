# Is this legal advice?

No. PDF Changer content is general security information and cannot replace legal advice tailored to your jurisdiction and situation.

## Why this matters

Legal risk varies by country, sector, employment contract, and the type of material involved.

## Safe default steps

1. Treat guides as technical education, not legal strategy.
2. If risk is serious, consult a qualified lawyer early.
3. Document your decisions and timeline for your own records.

## Common mistakes

- Assuming rules are the same across jurisdictions.
- Waiting too long to get legal counsel.
- Relying only on online advice in high-stakes cases.

## Limits

No website can evaluate your full legal context. Personalized legal guidance is essential when consequences are high.
