# Does a VPN make me anonymous?

No. A VPN can hide traffic from local networks, but it does not erase account, device, timing, or content-based identifiers.

## Why this matters

Many people overestimate VPN protection and skip other controls that matter more.

## Safe default steps

1. Treat VPN as one layer, not a full solution.
2. Combine network privacy with device and document hygiene.
3. Avoid identity-linked accounts during sensitive workflows.

## Common mistakes

- Logging into personal accounts while expecting anonymity.
- Reusing predictable timing and behavior patterns.
- Forgetting that document content can identify a source.

## Limits

A VPN shifts trust from one network to another provider. It does not eliminate traceability.
