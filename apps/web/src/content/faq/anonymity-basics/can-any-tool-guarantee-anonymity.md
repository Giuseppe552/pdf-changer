# Can any tool guarantee anonymity?

No. Good tools reduce risk, but anonymity can still fail through device monitoring, network logs, account links, and document content.

## Why this matters

Overconfidence causes mistakes. People skip checks when they believe one tool solved everything.

## Safe default steps

1. Use layered protection: device, network, and document.
2. Keep your workflow boring and repeatable.
3. Review the output file before sending it.
4. Share through channels designed for sensitive submissions.

## Common mistakes

- Treating metadata removal as a complete defense.
- Using personal or work accounts right after scrubbing.
- Ignoring visible clues in the document body.

## Limits

Anonymity is situational. The stronger your adversary and the more unique your behavior, the harder it is to stay unlinked.
