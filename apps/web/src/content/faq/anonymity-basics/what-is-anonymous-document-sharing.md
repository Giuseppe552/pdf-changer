# What is anonymous document sharing?

Anonymous document sharing means reducing the links between you, your device, your network, and the file so identification becomes harder.

## Why this matters

People usually focus only on the document. In real incidents, identity is often exposed by device logs, network logs, cloud sync, or visible clues in the content.

## Safe default steps

1. Treat anonymity as a full workflow, not a single tool.
2. Clean document metadata before sharing.
3. Avoid work devices and work networks when risk is high.
4. Use official secure channels from trusted newsrooms or legal organizations.

## Common mistakes

- Assuming "no uploads" alone guarantees anonymity.
- Forgetting that filenames and visible content can identify you.
- Sending from accounts tied to your real identity.

## Limits

No software can guarantee perfect anonymity. Risk depends on your environment, timing, and behavior.
