# Privacy Policy

**Effective:** 2026-02-18

## Overview

PDF Changer is built to minimize data collection. In v1, PDF tools run locally in your browser and **your PDF files do not upload to our servers**.

## What we collect

### Account data (passkeys)

If you create an account, we store:

- Passkey public keys (WebAuthn credentials)
- Passkey counters and basic timestamps (to prevent replay and keep auth secure)
- Your plan status (free/paid)
- Stripe customer/subscription identifiers (if you pay)

We do **not** require an email address or phone number for accounts.

### Newsletter (optional)

If you subscribe to product updates, we store the email address you provide for the purpose of sending those updates. We store newsletter emails encrypted at rest.

## What we do not collect

- We do not run third‑party analytics or tracking scripts.
- We do not use marketing pixels.
- We do not fingerprint your browser.
- We do not intentionally log or store your PDFs.

## IP addresses

Any web service on the internet will process IP addresses to deliver requests. We do not store IP addresses in our database and we do not build user profiles from IPs.

## Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. Stripe may process information required to complete the transaction. We store only the Stripe identifiers needed to reflect your subscription status.

## Data retention

We keep account and billing data for as long as you maintain an account, or as required to operate the service and comply with legal obligations.

## Security

We take steps to protect the service and the data we do store. However, no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

## Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent via the contact page.
