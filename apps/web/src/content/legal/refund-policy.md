# Refund Policy

**Effective:** 2026-04-15

## Paid plans

Paid plans are billed on a recurring basis (monthly unless otherwise stated) through Stripe. We do not store your card details.

## Your cancellation rights

### 14-day cooling-off period

Under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013, you have the right to cancel a digital content subscription within 14 days of purchase without giving any reason.

**However**, if you access the paid features of the service within the 14-day period, you acknowledge that:

- You have requested immediate access to the digital content
- You understand that by accessing it, you lose your 14-day right to cancel

This is consistent with Regulation 37(1)(a) of the 2013 Regulations. If you have not accessed any paid features during the 14-day period, you are entitled to a full refund.

### After the cooling-off period

You can cancel your subscription at any time via the Stripe billing portal. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are given for unused time within a billing period.

## Billing errors

If you believe you were charged in error (duplicate charge, charge after cancellation, incorrect amount), contact us with your Stripe receipt details. We will review the request and, where a billing error is confirmed, process a refund to the original payment method within 14 days.

## Refunds for service issues

If the service did not perform as described and you are a consumer in the United Kingdom, your rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 (sections 34–36, digital content) apply. If digital content is not of satisfactory quality, fit for purpose, or as described, you may be entitled to a repair, replacement, or refund as appropriate.

## How to request a refund

Email refunds@pdfchanger.org with:

- Your Stripe receipt or payment confirmation
- A description of the issue
- The date of the charge

We will respond within 7 days.
