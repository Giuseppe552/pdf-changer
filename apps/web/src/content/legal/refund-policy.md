# Refund Policy

**Effective:** 2026-02-18

## Subscriptions

Paid plans are billed on a recurring basis (monthly unless otherwise stated).

## Refunds

If you believe you were billed in error, contact us with your Stripe receipt details and we will review the request.

Refund decisions may depend on usage and timing. Where refunds are granted, they are processed back to the original payment method.

## Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription via the billing portal. Your access remains active until the end of the current billing period.
